About Us

More Than Just a Club

Founded in 2022, the Balboa Academy Science Club is a student-led community dedicated to exploring the wonders of science beyond the classroom.

Whether you're passionate about chemistry, biology, physics, or just curious about how the world works — our club is a space where curiosity is celebrated and discovery is shared.

Our Signature Events

Where the Action Happens

Annual

Science Trivia & Jeopardy

A high-energy academic showdown where Balboa Academy students go head-to-head answering science and math questions. With extra credit often on the line, it's as competitive as it is educational — and always a crowd favorite.

Balboa Academy All Grades
Outreach

School Invite

Every year, we host students from a low-income Panamanian school for a full day of immersive science — chemistry, biology, and physics stations, all designed to bring the thrill of discovery to students who otherwise wouldn't have access.

2025 Highlight

On October 28th, we welcomed La Escuela Bilingüe Kuna Negra for a day packed with erupting volcanoes, milk-and-soap reactions, the egg drop challenge, microscope investigations, and even a VR experience. Thanks to the @parentsassociationba for sponsoring, and to Ms. Mendoza for her generous donation of school supplies.

Balboa Academy Labs Oct 28, 2025
Competition

Battle of the Geniuses

What began in 2023 as an in-house Balboa Academy competition has grown into one of Panama's most exciting interschool academic events. Since 2024, hosted at Teatro Ateneo, Ciudad del Saber, four schools compete across math, science, general knowledge, music, history, arts, and geography — blending brainpower with teamwork.

Collaboration

Elementary STEAM Day

An annual tradition hosted by the high school Science Club in collaboration with the WiSTEM club. Elementary students dive into chemistry, biology, and physics activities designed to ignite early curiosity in STEM — helping them see science not as a subject, but as something they can do, love, and own.
